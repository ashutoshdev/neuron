# Neuron Manifesto

## Why am I building Neuron?

I am building Neuron because developing AI applications today is more difficult than it should be. Developers spend too much time connecting different tools instead of building real products.

I want to create a framework that makes AI application development simple, clean, and enjoyable, just like Django did for web development.

---

## What frustrations do I have with today's AI frameworks?

Today's AI frameworks often have these problems:

* Too many dependencies and libraries.
* Difficult to understand and learn.
* Tightly coupled to specific AI providers.
* Focused on demos instead of production applications.
* Frequent breaking changes.
* Developers spend more time on setup than solving business problems.

I want Neuron to solve these problems with a simple, stable, and production-ready architecture.

---

## What should developers feel when they use Neuron?

When developers use Neuron, they should feel:

* Productive from the first day.
* Confident that they are building on a solid foundation.
* Free to focus on their business logic instead of infrastructure.
* Happy because the framework is easy to understand.
* Comfortable knowing their code will continue to work as Neuron evolves.

The framework should feel natural, predictable, and enjoyable to use.

---

## What will Neuron never become?

Neuron will never become:

* A wrapper around a single AI provider.
* A collection of random AI utilities.
* A framework with unnecessary complexity.
* A framework that forces developers into one way of building applications.
* A project that breaks existing applications with every release.

Neuron will always value simplicity, stability, flexibility, and clean design.

---

## Where do I want Neuron to be in 10 years?

In 10 years, I want Neuron to be the first framework developers think of when building AI applications in Python.

I want it to have:

* A strong open-source community.
* Enterprise adoption around the world.
* Excellent documentation and learning resources.
* A stable and trusted ecosystem of plugins.
* A reputation for reliability, simplicity, and high quality.

My goal is for Neuron to become for AI applications what Django became for web applications—a framework that developers trust for building real production systems.
